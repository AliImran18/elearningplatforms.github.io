1. COURSERA:

Coursera is an online learning platform that offer more than 6700+ courses which includes short courses, specializations, professional certificates and also degree programs from prestigious universities such as STANFORD, DUKE, MICHIGAN and many more. The certificates of coursera are internationally recognized and plays a vital role in pacing up one's path and in developing and mastering a certain level of skill.

The price for individual courses varies from $29 to $99. However, there are thousands of courses that have the Audit option which allows you to take it for free. If you choose to audit the course, you won't receive graded assignments, a certificate, and bonus materials. Most courses consist of video lectures, assignments, reading materials, quizzes, projects, and group discussions.

But that's not the end, Coursera also offers a financial aid program through which you can complete a course and receive your certificate of completion for absolutely free..! The courses here are available in multiple languages such as English, Spanish, Chinese, Russian, Arabic, French, German and many more. Some of the course topics offered by coursera are "COMPUTER SCIENCE", "ARTS & HUMANITIES" "DATA SCIENCE", "MATH & LOGIC" and many more. 2. EDX:

The certified courses on edX may range from $50 to $300 but that's not the problem. EdX also offers financial aid on most of the courses through which one can receive upto 90% aid on a particular course. The courses on this platform are available on almost every topic such as "CHEMISTRY", "ART & CULTURE", "ELECTRONICS", "EDUCATION & TEACHER TRAINING", "MEDICINE", "FOOD & NUTRITION", "PHYSUCS", "COMPUTER SCIENCE", "MUSIC" and many more.
